ABOUT

About
Threefold

Here's the plain-English version of what we are, what we do, and whether this place might be for you.

The Short Version

What Is This?

Threefold is a text-to-play SCP roleplay server. You write your character's actions and dialogue in Discord channels, collaborating with other players to build scenes and storylines. Think of it like collaborative fiction with a bit of structure holding it together.

We're semi-serious. We love good lore and proper RP, but we're also a bunch of people who want to actually have fun. There's no entry exam. If you can write a sentence and get along with people, you'll fit in.

The Setting

We build on the SCP Foundation universe: the wiki, the anomalies, the factions. We don't follow any one specific canon. The wiki has hundreds of contradicting takes on the same stuff, and we try to stay inclusive to all of it rather than picking sides.

Mostly, though, Threefold is trying to be its own thing. We borrow the aesthetic and the broad strokes, then build from there. If a piece of wiki lore fits, great. If it doesn't, we don't force it.

Is This For You?

You'll Like It If...

You enjoy collaborative writing and want your choices to actually matter. Threefold is built around player-led lore. What your character does can shape the world, shift faction tensions, and change what future players walk into. Make a big enough impact and you might end up referenced on the server's own lore page.

If you're the kind of person who likes long threads, character arcs that go somewhere, and a setting that remembers what you did last week, you're in the right place.

Probably Not If...

You want a game rather than a story, or you're here to constantly win with no setbacks. Combat-RP slop, power-creeping, and "my character is untouchable" energy don't land well here. Defeats, complications, and losses are where the good writing actually happens.

We're not the right fit for everyone, and that's genuinely fine.
