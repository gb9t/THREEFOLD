Recycle Bin

Soft-deleted submissions and applications. Items are kept for 14 days from the moment of deletion, then permanently purged. During that window anyone with management access can restore an item back to its original list.

—

Restore behaviour: restoring re-creates the item in its original slot whenever possible. If another submission has since taken that slot number, a new one is assigned and the original is shown alongside for reference.

■

Owner-only action. The Purge Now button force-deletes an item immediately without waiting for the 14-day expiry. Only the Owner role can use this.
